The year is 2035 and a young urban archivist searches a vacant lot in South Philly. It is the site of the last row home. She discovers a collection of old cartoons, newspapers, and photographs. Excited by her discovery, she takes the images back to her lab for restoration. The images are unique not only in content but also in style. In order to share her discovery, the archivist uses her computer science skills to create a cartoon strip generator, pulling the styles from the found images and applying them to other photos.
