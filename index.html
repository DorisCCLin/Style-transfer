BACKGROUND:

In the distant future an urban archivist searches the rubble from the last row home in Philly. She finds a collection of photographs, cartoons, and films. To share her discovery, she creates a neural network that abstracts textures from the found footage and applies them to new images.

HOW TO USE:

  1. When the screen says “Ready”, choose a 1960’s style image to use as a texture.
  2. Click Animation to see the style texture applied to an animation.
  3. Repeat for each style image.

WEBCAM:

  1. Click Use Webcam to apply the style to you own images.
  2. You must click ACCEPT when to the webcam popup. No images will be stored or transmitted.
  3. Repeat for each style image.
